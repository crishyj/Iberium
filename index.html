How it Works
Sending payments from the United States to Latin America simplified and expedited using using Iberium.
Iberium accepts money from your bank account and moves it directly to your destination account.
Secure and smart contract validated transactions enabled using latest blockchain technology protocols.
IBER Pass
Treasury and Settlement Ecosystem
  • Real-time transaction processing
  • Receive payment instantly in local currency
  • Funds received directly into a mobile wallet, bank account or via cash-pickup
  • End-to-end mass payout processing
  • Access to our entire network through a single API connection
  • Full integration support with access to monitoring and reporting tools
IBER Pay
Tokenized cross border payment solutions to LATAM
  • Regular payments can be made in USD, EUR and received in local currency by country. Processing is automated for smaller and over-the-counter transactions directly with our negotiating table for large payments.
  • Send to multiple mobile and bank accounts, which facilitates the payment of payroll or multiple providers.
  • Access to our entire network through a single API connection
  • Full integration support with access to monitoring and reporting tools
